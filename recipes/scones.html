Scones


English scones to enjoy with cream and jam!


Ingredients

  • 350g self raising all-purpose-flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 85g butter
  • 3 tbsp caster sugar
  • 175ml milk

Instructions


  1. Mix self raising flour, salt and baking powder
  2. Add the butter and crumble. Stir in caster sugar
  3. Warm the milk and leave aside
  4. Make a well in the dry mix and add milk
  5. Roll and cut into circles
  6. Bake for 10 mins until golden
